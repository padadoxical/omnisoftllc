MANIFESTO

Secure log access for developers

Manifesto is a lightweight log access utility. It lets development teams view and search application log files without needing direct login to production servers or VM's.

  • 1 Faster troubleshooting for developers
  • 2 No RDP or shell access required
  • 3 Less “can you pull logs for me?” noise on infrastructure
